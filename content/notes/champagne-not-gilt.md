There is a difference between wearing gold and being trimmed in it.

Gilt shouts. It covers a surface to convince you of value it doesn't have. A single champagne line — one hairline, placed with intent — does the opposite. It doesn't cover anything. It *points*, quietly, and trusts you to look.

Restraint is the tell. The line is everything.
