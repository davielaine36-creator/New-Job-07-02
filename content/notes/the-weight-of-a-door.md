You can tell everything about a building from how its front door closes.

A cheap door rattles — it tells you where the money ran out. A considered one meets the frame with a low, certain *thunk*, damped and final, and something in you relaxes before you've consciously registered why.

The rest is just square footage. The door is the argument.
